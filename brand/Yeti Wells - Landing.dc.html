HOW PROOF WORKS · FOUR STEPS

From one tap to verified water.

01
Give, gasless

Sign in with Google (zkLogin). Donate in one tap. Enoki sponsors the gas — you never see a seed phrase, a wallet install, or a fee.

02
A machine reads reality

A registered TEE enclave reads the flow-meter and signs a milestone report with a key bound to its exact code — its measurements (PCRs) live on-chain.

03
The chain verifies, escrow releases

Move verifies the signature against the bound enclave. Change the code and the proof breaks. Funds release to the field partner; delivered liters advance.

04
Your proof fills

Your soulbound Impact NFT syncs from on-chain truth and fills with verified water. It can never be transferred — and never un-fills.
